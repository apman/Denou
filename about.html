The Denoue Vision

At Denoue, we’re on a mission to positively impact long-term care for everyone.

  • Long-term residential facilities are vital to our communities. They provide shelter and healthcare to specific populations, including the elderly and disabled, and they also provide jobs. Their success or failure to operate efficiently impacts not only the managers but also the aide workers and residents of these facilities.
  • The status quo of disparate software tools isn’t working for these businesses. Operators lack the time and experience to seek out technology solutions and yet they know they could greatly benefit from AI-assisted scheduling and task management, for example, to coordinate care across their facilities.
  • We are building a new type of system that works for long-term residential facilities. With Denoue’s help, it will be easier for administrators to manage operations in one place, schedule resources, and monitor the well-being of their residents. With Denoue, facility managers and aide workers can focus on what really matters: providing residents with the best possible care and support.
